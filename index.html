A few Words

Bakery Delights was founded in 2016 to bring the authentic taste of home baking to Guntur City, Andhra Pradesh. We offer and cater a wide range of baked treats and desserts including cakes, pastries, cupcakes, layer cakes, brownies, pies, cheesecakes, tarts, and many such delicacies fulfilling any event from birthdays to weddings.

We have revolutionized the gourmet of cupcakes and cakes; essentially our red velvet cakes are very renown in the city. On a regular basis we continue to innovate and customize variety of flavors to suit our customer needs.
